Episode 6 of Pokémon: Twilight Wings, the Galar Region Animated Series

You can now catch the sixth episode of Pokémon: Twilight Wings, the seven-episode limited animated series set in the Galar region, on Pokémon TV and the official Pokémon YouTube channel. The first five episodes are also available to watch so you can catch up on the whole story.

In the previous episode, we saw how Oleana keeps the Galar region running smoothly. This time, we're reintroduced to Tommy, who has just gotten into an argument with his friend John. In an attempt to make amends, Tommy enlists the help of Allister, the Galar region's Ghost-type Gym Leader. And you can bet that when Allister gets involved, things are going to end up…a bit weird. See for yourself what spookiness arises in this latest episode, titled “Moonlight.”

New Pokémon: Twilight Wings episodes are released monthly, so check back to find out when the next (and final) episode will premiere. Remember, you can catch the sixth episode of Pokémon: Twilight Wings now on Pokémon TV—here on Pokemon.com or using the Pokémon TV mobile app—or on the official Pokémon YouTube channel.
